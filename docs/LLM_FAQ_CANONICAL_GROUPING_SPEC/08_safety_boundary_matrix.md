## Safety Duplication Clusters

- disclaimer cluster: `faq_disclaimer_templates` vs `policy_override_disclaimer_templates` stay separate by live status and policy ownership.
- clarify and refuse cluster: `answer_readiness_gate_templates`, `required_core_facts_domain_clarify`, and `verify_candidate_clarify_templates` remain separate by trigger semantics.
- generic fallback cluster: `finalize_candidate_fallback_templates`, `paid_reply_guard_defaults`, and `webhook_assistant_top_level_templates` remain separate by layer and route.
- direct-answer overlap cluster: free contextual vs paid casual vs paid domain for school, banking, SSN, and related domain responses.
